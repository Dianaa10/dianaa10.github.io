About Me

Hello! I'm Diana, a budding software developer with an unyielding passion for all things tech. Hailing from the vibrant city of Los Angeles, California, I'm on a mission to carve my path in the dynamic world of software engineering. My journey began with Python, a language that ignited my fascination with coding. Since then, I've delved into the intricate realms of C and JavaScript, finding a special affinity for the elegant simplicity of C. Beyond coding, I'm deeply committed to fostering diversity and inclusion in STEM fields. You'll often find me actively engaged with organizations like the Society of Professional Hispanic Engineers (SHPE) and Women Who Code, where I volunteer my time to empower underrepresented communities in tech. I'm also proud to contribute to the educational initiatives at the Columbia Memorial Space Center, fueling young minds with the wonders of science and technology. To stay at the forefront of innovation, I make it a priority to immerse myself in industry events and tech conferences. From attending the React Summit to keeping tabs on the latest breakthroughs at TechCrunch Space, I'm always eager to absorb new knowledge and insights. In my pursuit of a career in software development, I bring not only technical skills but also a relentless enthusiasm for learning and growth. I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and eager to collaborate with like-minded individuals who share my passion for pushing the boundaries of what technology can achieve.
